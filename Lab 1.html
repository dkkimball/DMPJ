Stock Dog Trialing is my Hobby

(Mostly the dogs let me come along because I feed them...)



My hobby is stock dog trialing. I have two border collies that I compete with, Bert who is old and wise(i.e. he is smarter than me,) and Henry who is young and exuberant(i.e. he doesn't listen to me!) In the summer we travel around Colorado to dog trials where we compete against hundreds of other handlers and dogs. While we love to compete our favorite part of any trial is setting out the sheep for other competitors. This requires us to take the sheep to a designated spot where the dog competing will come and gather them and then take them through the set course. Every trial is a fantasic get together as handlers and dogs come from all around the world, and you never know what new friends you will meet!




My Favorite Dog Trials are:

  • Soldier Hollow - Park City, Utah
  • Meeker - Meeker, Colorado
  • Table Top - Black Forest, Colorado
  • National Finals - Carbondale, Colorado
